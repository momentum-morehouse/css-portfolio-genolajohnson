About Me

Hi, I’m Genola Johnson. I’ve successfully found my purpose. Originally, I didn’t want to be in education, however, it found me. I wanted to help others. And, I really enjoy teaching. I liked being about to create systems and processes for students to understand a concept. Somehow, my purpose got lost in the shuffle of weekly lesson plans aligning to a test that I wasn’t even sure would actually help my students become productive citizens in today’s global society. Barely touching on concepts that I deeply felt would come back to hurt the students and, “my test scores”, because they didn’t get the foundational skills needed to move to the next level. And quite frankly, I felt lost and out of control.

That’s when I began the long, long journey of really finding my purpose. Once I found it, I was really happy, I didn’t hate my job anymore and was excited to return to work each day because I knew what I had decided to do after work, would help me reach my purpose. Now, I’m working and living in my purpose, and I want to help you find your purposeful profession.

So, how did I find my purpose? Like I said, it was a long journey. I had created businesses, some failed, some succeeded and ran their course, and others I quit before it had time to mature. Each time, I would network, people would ask, how did you do this? I want to do what you are doing. Okay, if you want to do what I’m doing, you’re going to have to take the steps I took to get there. Hopefully, I can help you not take as long and as many detours like I did.

Has it been fun? Yes, Has it been scary? YES! But, at the end of the day, I know the journey of getting to your purpose is what really matters. Now, I want to share my successes and failures with others. I want to share how I found my purpose as well as how I found the avenues to work in my purpose. That’s where the joy comes from. Finding work that doesn’t feel like work.

This site is here for two purposes:

  1. Guiding you to finding your purpose;
  2. Helping you find work to help you fulfill your purpose.
